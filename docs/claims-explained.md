# Claims Explained

This page explains the patent at a high level. It does not provide legal interpretation.

## Main Claim Area

The central claim area concerns mixtures of nucleotides, including dNTPs and/or analogues, where the nucleotide components may be present in specific non-equimolar proportions or concentrations.

## Functional Use

The mixtures are described for use in nucleic acid polymerization workflows, including amplification and sequencing.

## Practical Meaning

In practical terms, the patent treats nucleotide mixture composition as an adjustable parameter. Instead of assuming that every target should be amplified or sequenced with a standard equimolar nucleotide mixture, the invention proposes matching or adapting the mixture to the nucleotide composition of the target polymer.
