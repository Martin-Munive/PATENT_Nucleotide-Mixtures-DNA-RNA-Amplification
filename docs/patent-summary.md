# Patent Summary

## Patent Family

This repository documents the public patent family associated with:

- **CO2017004500A1**
- **WO2018203280A1**

The invention is titled **Nucleotide mixtures for amplification and sequencing of nucleic acid polymers**.

## Scientific Problem

Nucleic acid amplification and sequencing workflows commonly use nucleotide mixtures where dNTPs are present at equimolar concentrations. That convention is practical, but it does not necessarily reflect the nucleotide composition of every DNA or RNA target.

Templates with different nucleotide distributions may benefit from adjusted nucleotide proportions during polymerization. The patent addresses that problem by proposing mixtures whose nucleotide concentrations or molar proportions can be adapted to the target nucleic acid polymer.

## Core Idea

The invention describes nucleotide mixtures, including dNTPs and/or analogues, where the components are not necessarily present in equal proportions. These mixtures are intended for amplification, reverse transcription, cDNA synthesis and sequencing workflows.
