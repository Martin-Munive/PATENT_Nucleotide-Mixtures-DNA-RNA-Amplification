# Inventor Record

## Public Identity Connection

The GitHub profile name is:

**Martin Munive**

The patent records list the coinventor as:

**Nestor Martin Munive Arguelles**

This repository uses the following formulation:

> Martin Munive is listed in the patent records as Nestor Martin Munive Arguelles.

## Why This Wording Matters

Patent records often use formal legal names, while public software and scientific profiles may use a shorter professional name. The wording above connects both identities without making claims beyond the public record.

## Appropriate Public Claim

Appropriate:

- listed coinventor;
- participant in the patent record;
- public documentation of a patent family where Martin Munive appears as Nestor Martin Munive Arguelles.

Avoid:

- sole inventor;
- patent owner;
- individual assignee;
- creator of the whole invention without qualification;
- legal status claims not verified from official records.

## Institutional Assignees

The patent record should be consulted for the authoritative assignee list. The public patent family identifies institutional assignees, not individual ownership by a single inventor.
